# Return Policy

## General Return Window
Customers may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Items must be in their original condition, including all original packaging, accessories, manuals, and warranty cards. Proof of purchase is required for all returns.

## Electronics Returns
Electronics may be returned within 30 days of delivery provided the item is unused and in its original sealed packaging. Once the seal is broken, electronics are only eligible for return if the item is defective or does not match its description. A 10% restocking fee applies to opened, non-defective electronics.

## Defective or Damaged Items
If your item arrives defective or damaged, contact us within 7 days of delivery. We will arrange a free return shipment and issue a full refund or send a replacement at no additional cost. Photographs of the damage may be requested.

## Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned: opened software or digital licenses, consumables (batteries, ink cartridges) once used, custom or personalized items, and items marked as final sale. Gift cards are non-refundable.

## Refund Processing
Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the returned item. The refund will be issued to the original payment method. Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

## How to Initiate a Return
Log into your account, navigate to Order History, select the order, and click "Return Item". You will receive a prepaid return label by email within 24 hours. Pack the item securely and drop it off at any authorized shipping location.
