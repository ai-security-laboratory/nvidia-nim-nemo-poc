# Shipping Policy

## Standard Shipping
Standard shipping is available on all orders and takes 5–7 business days. Orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping. For orders under $50, standard shipping costs $5.99.

## Express Shipping
Express shipping delivers within 2–3 business days and costs $12.99 regardless of order size. Express shipping is available for most items except large or heavy products.

## Overnight Shipping
Overnight delivery is available for orders placed before 2:00 PM local time on business days. Cost is $24.99. Not available for large appliances, furniture, or hazardous materials.

## Free Shipping Threshold
All orders over $50 automatically qualify for free standard shipping. Gold and Platinum loyalty members receive free standard shipping on all orders regardless of order total.

## Shipping Restrictions
We currently ship to all 50 US states. International shipping is not available at this time. P.O. boxes are supported for standard shipping only. We do not ship to freight forwarders.

## Order Processing Time
Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed the next business day. You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.

## Large Item Delivery
Large items such as televisions over 55 inches, monitors, and computer workstations are delivered via freight carrier. Delivery takes 7–14 business days and includes curbside delivery. White-glove in-home delivery is available for an additional $49.
