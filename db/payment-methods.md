# Payment Methods

## Accepted Payment Methods
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover credit and debit cards. We also accept PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption and 3D Secure authentication where supported.

## Buy Now, Pay Later
We offer Buy Now, Pay Later through Klarna and Afterpay. Split your purchase into 4 interest-free payments. Available for orders between $35 and $2,000. A soft credit check may be performed. Late payment fees apply as per the provider's terms.

## Store Credit
Store credit is issued for returns and can be applied to any future purchase. Store credit does not expire and can be combined with loyalty points. Store credit cannot be transferred or converted to cash.

## Gift Cards
Physical and digital gift cards are available in denominations of $25, $50, $100, and $200. Gift cards can be used online and in-store. They cannot be reloaded or used to purchase other gift cards. Lost or stolen gift cards cannot be replaced.

## Payment Security
We do not store full credit card numbers. All card data is tokenized through our payment processor. We are PCI DSS Level 1 compliant. If you notice an unauthorized charge, contact us immediately and we will initiate a dispute on your behalf.

## Invoicing for Business Customers
Business accounts with an approved credit application can pay by invoice with Net-30 terms. Contact our business sales team to apply. Minimum order value for invoice payment is $500.
