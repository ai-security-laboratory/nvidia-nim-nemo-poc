# Loyalty Program

## Overview
The RetailBot Rewards program lets you earn points on every purchase. Points can be redeemed for discounts on future orders. Membership is free and automatic with account creation.

## Earning Points
You earn 1 point for every $1 spent. Points are credited to your account within 24 hours of order delivery. Bonus points promotions run monthly — follow our newsletter to stay informed. Points do not expire as long as your account has activity within 12 months.

## Loyalty Tiers
- **Bronze**: 0–999 lifetime points. Standard benefits.
- **Silver**: 1,000–4,999 lifetime points. Free standard shipping on all orders, early access to sales.
- **Gold**: 5,000–14,999 lifetime points. Free standard shipping, priority customer support, 5% discount on all purchases, birthday bonus (double points in your birthday month).
- **Platinum**: 15,000+ lifetime points. Free express shipping on all orders, dedicated account manager, 10% discount on all purchases, exclusive access to new product launches, quarterly gift.

## Redeeming Points
Points can be redeemed at checkout. 100 points = $1 discount. Minimum redemption is 500 points ($5). Points cannot be combined with other discount codes. Redeemed points are non-refundable.

## Points on Returns
If you return an item for a refund, the points earned on that purchase will be deducted from your account. If a return brings your balance below a tier threshold, your tier will be adjusted at the next quarterly review.

## Referral Bonus
Earn 500 bonus points for every friend you refer who makes their first purchase. Your friend also receives a $10 welcome discount. There is no limit to the number of referrals.
