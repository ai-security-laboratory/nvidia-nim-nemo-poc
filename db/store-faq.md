# Store FAQ

## Can I change or cancel my order?
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters our fulfillment pipeline and cannot be changed. Once shipped, you will need to wait for delivery and then initiate a return if needed.

## Do you offer price matching?
Yes. We match the current listed price of identical items sold and shipped by major authorized retailers including Amazon, Best Buy, and B&H. The item must be in stock at the competitor at the time of the request. Contact customer support with a link to the competitor's listing.

## How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting Order History. Tracking updates may take up to 24 hours to appear after the label is created.

## What if my item is out of stock?
You can sign up for back-in-stock notifications on any product page. We will email you as soon as the item is available again. For high-demand items, we recommend acting quickly as stock can sell out fast.

## Do you have a physical store?
Currently we operate exclusively online. Our warehouse and customer support team are based in Seattle, WA. We do not offer local pickup at this time.

## How do I contact customer support?
Customer support is available Monday–Friday 8 AM–8 PM PT and Saturday 9 AM–5 PM PT. You can reach us via live chat on our website, by email at support@retailbot.com, or by phone at 1-800-555-0100. Gold and Platinum loyalty members have access to priority support with shorter wait times.

## Is my personal data safe?
We take data privacy seriously. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your data is stored securely and used only to process orders, improve your shopping experience, and comply with legal requirements. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
