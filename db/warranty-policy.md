# Warranty Policy

## Manufacturer Warranty
All products sold in our store come with the manufacturer's standard warranty. The warranty period varies by product category: laptops and computers carry a 1-year warranty, smartphones a 1-year warranty, headphones and audio equipment a 1-year warranty, and accessories such as cables and hubs a 90-day warranty.

## Extended Warranty Plans
We offer extended warranty plans for electronics at checkout. A 2-year extended plan costs 10% of the product price. A 3-year extended plan costs 15% of the product price. Extended warranties cover mechanical and electrical failures under normal use. They do not cover accidental damage, liquid damage, or cosmetic damage.

## Warranty Claims Process
To file a warranty claim, contact our support team with your order number and a description of the issue. For manufacturing defects within the first 30 days, we will replace the item at no charge. After 30 days and within the warranty period, the manufacturer handles repairs or replacements directly.

## What Is Not Covered
Warranties do not cover: physical damage caused by drops or impacts, liquid damage, unauthorized modifications or repairs, normal wear and tear, cosmetic damage that does not affect functionality, and damage caused by using the product outside its intended purpose.

## MacBook and Apple Products
Apple products carry Apple's standard limited warranty and are eligible for AppleCare+ extended coverage, which can be purchased directly from Apple within 60 days of purchase. Our store offers assistance with AppleCare+ enrollment at no additional fee.

## Warranty for Refurbished Items
Certified refurbished products carry a 90-day warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Extended warranty plans are not available for refurbished items.
